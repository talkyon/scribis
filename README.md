# Scribis: Speech to Text

[English](README.md) | [繁體中文](README-zh.md)

**Transcribe, Translate & Notes — Reimagining Your Audio Transcription and AI Collaboration Experience**

Scribis is a high-performance local AI speech-to-text tool designed for privacy and efficiency. It leverages on-device processing to turn every audio clip into a valuable knowledge asset without ever uploading your data to the cloud.

> **Note**: This repository is used for hosting binary releases, documentation, and issue tracking. The source code of Scribis is not public.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎙️ Support for Leading Local AI Models
Scribis supports top-tier models for diverse transcription needs, ensuring high accuracy and speed without internet dependency.
- **OpenAI Whisper**: Precision recognition supporting 99+ languages.
- **SenseVoice Small**: Ultra-fast multilingual recognition for Chinese, English, Japanese, and Korean.
- **VibeVoice & Voxtral**: Specialized models for high-fidelity audio processing.
- **Qwen 3 ASR & Parakeet**: Deeply optimized for multilingual contexts and lightning-fast low latency.

![Multiple Local ASR Models](images/en/MULTIPLE%20LOCAL%20ASR%20MODELS-1-0-16.png)

### 🔍 Precision Video OCR
Extract and digitize hardcoded (burnt-in) subtitles directly from videos with our custom zone framing tool. 
- **macOS**: Powered by native Apple OCR for seamless performance.
- **Windows**: Supports Tesseract and PaddleOCR for high-precision extraction.

![OCR Text Extraction](images/en/OCR%20TEXT%20EXTRACTION.png)

### ⚡ Real-time Transcription & App-Specific Capture
- **App-Specific Audio Capture**: Filter background noise and capture high-quality audio directly from specific applications (e.g., Zoom, Teams, Chrome).
- **Floating Subtitles**: View live captions in a floating widget as you record or attend meetings.
- **Global Translation**: Break language barriers with real-time translated subtitles for international calls or lectures.

![App-Specific Audio Capture](images/en/APP-SPECIFIC%20AUDIO%20CAPTURE.png)
![Real-Time Floating Subtitles](images/en/REAL-TIME%20FLOATING%20SUBTITLES.png)

### 👥 Speaker Diarization
- **Automatic Role Separation**: Advanced algorithms distinguish between different speakers in an audio file.
- **Structured Transcripts**: Automatically labels "who said what and when," keeping your transcripts organized for meetings or interviews.

![Local Speaker Audio Redesign](images/en/LOCAL%20SPEAKER%20AUDIO%20REDESIGN.png)

### 🤖 AI Smart Assistant (Local LLM Support)
- **Local GGUF Integration**: Use local LLMs for offline summaries and interactive chat with your audio content.
- **Smart Summaries**: Turn hours of recordings into concise summaries and actionable insights in seconds.

![Local LLM GGUF Support](images/en/LOCAL%20LLM%20GGUF%20SUPPORT.png)

### 📝 Efficient Subtitle Editor
Fine-tune your transcripts with a built-in editor designed for speed and accuracy. Easily adjust timing and text before exporting.

![Subtitle Editor](images/en/SUBTITLE%20EDITOR.png)

### 🔊 Text-to-Speech (TTS)
- **Natural Voice Synthesis**: Built-in CPU-powered TTS technology providing human-like voice options for English, Chinese, and Japanese.
- **Convert Text to Speech**: Ideal for video voiceovers, audiobook creation, or reading assistance.

### 🛠️ Professional Workflow Integration
- **Multi-Format Export**: Support for SRT, VTT, Markdown, and Plain Text.
- **Media Library**: Manage thousands of projects with global search and efficient tagging.
